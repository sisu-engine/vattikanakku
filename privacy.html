Privacy Policy

Vatti Kanakku (வட்டி கணக்கு)

Last Updated: December 25, 2024

TL;DR: Your financial data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information. The only data collected is anonymous usage data by Google AdMob for showing relevant ads.

1. Introduction

Vatti Kanakku ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

By using Vatti Kanakku, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Financial Data

We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit your financial data to any server. All your data including:

  • Lending and borrowing records
  • Transaction history
  • Promissory notes
  • Contact information
  • Interest calculations

is stored locally on your device only using SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it (via PDF export or sharing features).

2.2 Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Device advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage data for ad personalization
  • Approximate location (country/city level only)

This data is collected and managed by Google according to their Privacy Policy.

2.3 Analytics Data

We use PostHog to understand how users interact with our app. PostHog collects anonymous usage data including:

  • App screens viewed (e.g., "Home Screen", "Ledger List")
  • Feature usage patterns (e.g., "EMI Calculator used", "Person Added")
  • Anonymous interaction events (button clicks, navigation)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and language preference

Important: PostHog does NOT collect any personal financial data, transaction amounts, person names, or any personally identifiable information. All analytics data is anonymized and used solely to improve app features and user experience.

PostHog data is processed according to their Privacy Policy.

2.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Precise location data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Contact list (beyond what you manually enter in the app)
  • Any other personally identifiable information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Local Data Usage

Your financial data stored locally is used solely for:

  • Displaying your lending/borrowing records
  • Calculating interest
  • Generating promissory notes and PDFs
  • Providing app functionality

3.2 Advertising

Google AdMob uses collected data to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

4.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
    Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your financial data remains on your device. The only data shared is anonymous advertising data with Google AdMob as described above.

4.3 PDF Sharing

When you choose to share generated PDFs (promissory notes, reports), you control where this data goes. We use the device's native sharing functionality, and the data is only shared with apps you explicitly choose (e.g., WhatsApp, Email).

5. Data Security

We take your data security seriously:

  • All data is stored locally using encrypted SQLite database
  • No internet connection is required for core app functionality
  • No cloud servers or external databases are used
  • Only you have access to your financial data on your device

However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. The security of your device (screen lock, device encryption) is your responsibility.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists as long as you keep the app installed
  • You can delete individual records within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your data
  • We have no way to recover your data once deleted

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or update any records
  • Delete: You can delete individual records or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Export: You can generate and share PDFs of your records
  • Opt-out of Ads: You can reset your advertising ID in your device settings

9. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: Required for displaying AdMob advertisements only
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check internet connectivity for ads

Note: We do NOT request access to your camera, microphone, location, photos, or contacts.

10. International Users

This app is designed for users globally. Since all data is stored locally on your device, it remains in your geographical location. Only advertising data may be processed by Google's servers, which may be located in different countries.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting an in-app notification (for major changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted.

12. Third-Party Links

Our app does not contain links to other websites. If we add such features in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@vatikanakku.com (or your preferred email)
  • In-App: Use the "Contact Support" option in app settings

We will respond to your inquiries within 7 business days.

14. Consent

By using Vatti Kanakku, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the app.
